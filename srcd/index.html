Throttle (Leading)

Executes immediately, then ignores all events for the duration.

Debounce (Trailing)

Executes only after you stop typing for the duration.

Debounce (Leading) - Input

Executes immediately, then ignores events until a quiet period passes.

Debounce (Leading) - Button

The first click succeeds, subsequent rapid clicks are ignored.

Debounce by Click Count

The first click succeeds, then the next 3 clicks are ignored.
